Audio Video Club

The Audio Video Club provides hands-on experience in audio recording, video editing, and filmmaking. We host workshops on cutting-edge technology and techniques, allowing members to create compelling content and enhance technical skills. Our activities include film screenings, production projects, and collaborations, fostering a creative and innovative environment for audiovisual enthusiasts.
