University Admission Details

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)
  • Bachelor of Arts (B.A)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
  • Master of Science (M.Sc)
  • Master of Arts (M.A)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Seat Configuration

  • B.Sc: 100 seats
  • B.A: 80 seats
  • B.Com: 60 seats
  • M.Sc: 50 seats
  • M.A: 40 seats
  • MBA: 30 seats

Admission Criteria

  • Entrance Exam: All candidates must appear for the university's entrance exam.
  • Merit-Based: Selection will be based on the merit list prepared after the entrance exam.
  • Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview.

Eligibility

  • For Undergraduate Courses: Minimum 50% marks in 12th standard from a recognized board.
  • For Postgraduate Courses: Minimum 50% marks in the relevant undergraduate course from a recognized university.
  • Age Limit: The candidate must be at least 17 years old on the 1st of July of the admission year.
